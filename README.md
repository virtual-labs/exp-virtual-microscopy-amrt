### About the Experiment 

This is a virtual simulation focused on a high-fidelity environment for students to master the operation of a compound light microscope, a foundational tool in pathology and microbiology. Through the interactive interface, users can simulate essential laboratory techniques such as adjusting the coarse and fine focus, controlling the light intensity through the condenser and iris diaphragm, and switching between different objective lenses (e.g., 10x, 40x, and 100x oil immersion). The simulation allows for the observation of various biological specimens, enabling students to calculate total magnification and understand the relationship between magnification and the field of view. By replicating the tactile experience of slide manipulation and focal adjustment, this virtual lab ensures that students develop technical proficiency and a conceptual understanding of optics before transitioning to physical laboratory equipment.
